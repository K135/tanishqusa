Our Story

For over 2 decades, Tanishq has brightened many occasions for women across India with its range of jewelry in gold and diamond. Through unmatched artistry, quality and an unshakable trust in TATA, Tanishq continues to be a household name across India, with over 350 store, in addition to the UAE.

At Tanishq, you will know what you’re paying for, as we maintain complete transparency in billing. So, you don’t have to worry about any hidden charges with us.

Every piece of jewelry you take home is-brand new and unworn. We religiously melt every Tanishq jewellery piece we exchange and never refurbish it.

At Tanishq you are charged only for the actual weight of gold after subtracting the weight of stones from the total weight of the jewel. This way, you are never charged the price of gold for the weight of stones.

Every Tanishq store has the state-of-the-art karatmeter to accurately, measure the purity of gold and cement that our gold is as pure as we say it is.

Tanishq is a Tata product and comes with a timeless and inimitable trust that you can hold on to, forever.
